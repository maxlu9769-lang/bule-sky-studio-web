# ✈️ Blue Sky Studio

> **From 0 to 1: Exploring Aerospace Engineering and Digital Media Arts.**

This is not just a gaming club. We are a learning community that combines **Real Flight Simulation**, **Aviation Theory**, and **Professional Video Editing**.

---

## 📚 What We Teach

### 1. 🎬 Professional Video Editing (DaVinci Resolve)
- **Start from Zero**: From importing raw footage to exporting 4K videos.
- **Color Science**: Why do movies look better? Learn how to create a cinematic look.
- **Editing Logic**: How to tell stories with shots instead of just piling up clips.

### 2. ✈️ Aviation Knowledge & Flight Sim
- **How to Pilot**: From Cessna 172 to commercial airliners.
- **Aerodynamics**: Why do wings generate lift?
- **Practical Exercises**: Experience real Vancouver flight routes in simulators.
